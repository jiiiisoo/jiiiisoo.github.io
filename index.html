Research

My research focuses on developing general and scalable approaches for real-world perception, reasoning, and action. I’m broadly interested in physical intelligence, including topics such as 3D motion, video perception & generation and robot action generation. Recently, I’m excited about leveraging foundation models for grounded interaction and action policy learning.
